About

This is what we do.

Founded and led by Ambuj Nayar, the studio has evolved as a multidisciplinary practice with a rapidly growing eclectic portfolio of projects comprising of Architecture, Interiors, Planning and Engineering. Our Principal Ambuj Nayar graduated from MACT, Maulana Azad College of Technology (now MANIT) from the faculty of Architecture, REC Bhopal in the year 1991. The ANAPL architectural practice was started in New Delhi in 1991 and moved to its present location in Gurgaon, the hub of all types of architectural and interior design activity, in 2001.
As both Commercial and Residential Architects and Interior designers, our range of work includes IT Buildings, Corporate offices, Factory Buildings, Residences, Offices Interiors, Residential Interiors, Retail Interiors, Farm Houses and Schools. Our practice is committed to providing architecture of a high caliber. We are driven by a consistent philosophical approach, not a predetermined style. We embrace modernist architecture, striving to produce clean, spacious and light designs. As Architects we believe that high quality design results from the continuity of the design process and the intensive dialogue with the client, consultants, contractors and end users. We work closely together to ensure the best results are achieved through the synthesis of concept, beauty and functional integration. ANAPL is a design firm offering sensitive and innovative solutions across spectrum of wide and diverse user groups and markets in the field of IT buildings, Corporate offices, Industrial buildings, Residences, Schools, Banks, Retail outlets and Furniture.
Our mission is to provide a value added service to businesses and individuals by managing projects to cost and quality standards agreed with the client. We are an experienced and committed team of professionals with the expertise to guarantee the smooth and cost efficient running of your project. The consistency of return customers is testimony to our ability to interpret client desires, control costs, meet deadlines and control all elements of the construction process. Our list of projects is our best list of references. With our proven experience in our professional capabilities, we feel we can best provide the solution for our clients' building and planning concerns. To our capabilities we add our insistence on responsive communication, adherence to schedules and budget concerns and a commitment to design excellence.
